### H2 Section 3: Supplier Evaluation - The 5 Critical Factors (450-500 words)
**Keyword target:** "phone parts supplier", "supplier evaluation"

#### H3: Factor 1 - Defect Rate & Quality Control
- Industry benchmarks: <50 PPM (excellent), 50-100 PPM (acceptable), >200 PPM (avoid)
- Ask for: Quality control documentation, testing protocols
- Red flag: Suppliers who can't provide defect rate data

#### H3: Factor 2 - Warranty Terms & RMA Process
- Top-tier: 3-year warranty, 0 deductible
- Mid-tier: 2-year warranty, small deductible
- Budget: 1-year warranty, full deductible
- Key question: "What's your RMA turnaround time?"

#### H3: Factor 3 - Support & Response Time
- Critical issue response: 2-4 hours (good) vs 24+ hours (poor)
- Remote diagnostics capability
- On-site support availability

#### H3: Factor 4 - Inventory & Fulfillment
- Same-day shipping capability
- Stock depth for popular models
- Backorder handling

#### H3: Factor 5 - Pricing Transparency
- No hidden fees
- Clear MOQ requirements
- Volume discount structure

**Insider tip:** "The one-meter counter isn't selling products, it's selling information asymmetry"
