## Insider Knowledge to Include

### Chinese Market Terminology
1. **原装电池 (yuánzhuāng diànchí)** - Original/genuine battery
2. **高仿电池 (gāo fǎng diànchí)** - High-quality imitation battery
3. **虚标容量 (xū biāo róngliàng)** - Falsely labeled capacity (capacity fraud)
4. **循环次数 (xúnhuán cìshù)** - Cycle count/battery cycles
5. **保护板 (bǎohù bǎn)** - Protection circuit board (PCB)
6. **电芯 (diàn xīn)** - Battery cell (the core component)
7. **A品/B品电芯** - A-grade/B-grade cells
8. **鼓包 (gǔ bāo)** - Battery swelling
