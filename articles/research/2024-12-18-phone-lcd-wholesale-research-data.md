## Supplier Red Flags
1. No clear warranty (30 days minimum is standard)
2. Prices significantly below market average ($15+ under = likely counterfeit)
3. No certifications (ISO 9001, RoHS, CE marking)
4. Vague product descriptions ("high copy", "premium aftermarket")
5. No serialization data or traceability records
6. "All sales final" policies

## Supplier Green Flags
1. ISO 9001 certification
2. RoHS compliance
3. CE marking
4. Clear return policies (30+ days)
5. Batch testing documentation
6. Verifiable business registration
7. Long-term industry presence (5+ years)
