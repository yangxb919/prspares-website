1. **原装屏 (yuánzhuāng píng)** - Original/genuine screen
2. **后压屏 (hòu yā píng)** - Aftermarket refurbished glass on OEM display
3. **国产屏 (guóchǎn píng)** - Domestic/Chinese-made screen
4. **混货 (hùn huò)** - Mixed quality batches
