## Quality Grade Definitions

### OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)
- 100% original quality
- Manufactured by authorized partners
- Highest price point
- Best compatibility after updates

### Refurbished Original
- Original panels from damaged devices
- Professional restoration process
- 70-90% of original price
- Quality depends on refurbishment facility

## Supplier Evaluation Criteria

### Key Factors
1. **Defect Rate** - Target <50 PPM for premium, <100 PPM acceptable
2. **Warranty Terms** - Top: 3-year, 0 deductible; Budget: 1-year, full deductible
3. **Support Response** - Top: 2-hour on-site; Budget: 24+ hours
4. **Customization** - Top: 2 spec changes free; Budget: Limited
5. **MOQ Requirements** - Varies from 50K to 150K units for custom orders

### Total Cost of Ownership Calculation
For 100,000 units annually:
- LG ($20/panel, 50 PPM): ~5,000 defects → Lower total cost
- BOE ($16/panel, 100 PPM): ~10,000 defects → ~$8,000/month hidden costs
- Budget ($12/panel, 250 PPM): ~25,000 defects → Highest total cost
