### Aftermarket Screen Grades Explained
### 第三方屏幕等级详解

---

When sourcing replacement screens, you'll encounter several quality grades. Understanding these is crucial for your iPhone OLED test process:

在采购替换屏幕时，你会遇到几个质量等级。理解这些对你的iPhone OLED测试流程至关重要：

---

**OEM/Original:** Genuine Apple parts, either new or refurbished from original devices. Highest quality, highest price, limited availability.

**OEM/原装：** 正品苹果零件，可以是全新的，也可以是从原装设备上翻新的。最高质量，最高价格，供应有限。

---

**Soft OLED:** Flexible OLED panels that closely mimic original Apple displays. These offer the best aftermarket quality with proper color accuracy and viewing angles. They're more expensive but worth it for premium repairs.

**柔性OLED（软屏）：** 柔性OLED面板，与原装苹果显示屏高度相似。这类屏幕提供最佳的第三方质量，色彩准确度和可视角度都很好。价格较高，但对于高端维修来说物有所值。

---

**Hard OLED:** Rigid OLED panels that are more affordable than soft OLED. Good quality overall, but may have slightly different color temperature and viewing angle performance.

**硬性OLED（硬屏）：** 刚性OLED面板，比柔性OLED更实惠。整体质量良好，但色温和可视角度表现可能略有不同。

---

**Incell LCD:** LCD technology with integrated touch layer. No burn-in risk, but lower contrast ratios and no true blacks. A budget-friendly option for price-sensitive customers.

**Incell LCD：** 集成触控层的LCD技术。没有烧屏风险，但对比度较低，无法显示真正的纯黑。适合对价格敏感的客户的经济选择。

---

**TFT LCD:** Basic LCD technology. Lowest quality tier with noticeable differences in color and brightness. Only suitable for budget repairs where customers prioritize price over quality.

**TFT LCD：** 基础LCD技术。最低质量等级，色彩和亮度差异明显。仅适合客户更看重价格而非质量的预算维修。

---

The price difference between grades can be significant—a soft OLED might cost $60-80 wholesale while a TFT LCD costs $20-30. But here's what matters: your RMA rate on cheap screens will eat those savings alive.

不同等级之间的价格差异可能很大——柔性OLED批发价可能在60-80美元，而TFT LCD只要20-30美元。但关键是：劣质屏幕的返修率会把省下的钱全部吃掉。

## The Black Screen Test - Your First Line of Defense
## 黑屏测试——你的第一道防线

---

The black screen test is the simplest yet most revealing test you can perform on any iPhone OLED screen. It takes 30 seconds and can save you thousands in returns.

黑屏测试是你可以在任何iPhone OLED屏幕上进行的最简单却最能说明问题的测试。只需30秒，却能为你节省数千元的退货损失。

---

### Why This Test Matters
### 为什么这个测试很重要

---

OLED technology works fundamentally differently from LCD. In an OLED display, each pixel produces its own light. When displaying black, those pixels turn completely off—no light, no power consumption, true black.

OLED技术的工作原理与LCD有根本性的不同。在OLED显示屏中，每个像素都能自己发光。显示黑色时，这些像素完全关闭——没有光，没有功耗，真正的纯黑。

---

LCD screens, on the other hand, use a backlight that's always on. Even when displaying "black," light bleeds through, creating a grayish appearance.

另一方面，LCD屏幕使用的背光总是亮着的。即使显示"黑色"，光线也会透出，产生灰蒙蒙的外观。

---

This difference is your secret weapon for quality testing.

这种差异就是你进行质量检测的秘密武器。

---

### Step-by-Step Black Screen Test Procedure
### 黑屏测试步骤详解

---

1. **Prepare the environment:** Find a dark room or create one. Ambient light will interfere with your results.

1. **准备环境：** 找一个暗室或营造一个。环境光会干扰你的测试结果。

---

2. **Display pure black:** Use a website like darkblackscreen.com or the Doctor OLED X app ($1.99 on App Store). These tools display a pure black image across the entire screen.

2. **显示纯黑：** 使用darkblackscreen.com网站或Doctor OLED X应用（App Store售价1.99美元）。这些工具可以在整个屏幕上显示纯黑图像。

---

3. **Set brightness to maximum:** This is counterintuitive, but maximum brightness reveals defects that hide at lower levels.

3. **将亮度调到最高：** 这听起来违反直觉，但最高亮度能暴露在低亮度下隐藏的缺陷。

---

4. **Examine the screen carefully:** Look for:
   - Any glow or light leakage (indicates LCD, not OLED)
   - Uneven dark areas (mura defects)
   - Bright spots or dead pixels
   - Color tinting in supposedly black areas

4. **仔细检查屏幕：** 注意观察：
   - 任何发光或漏光（说明是LCD而不是OLED）
   - 不均匀的暗区（云纹缺陷）
   - 亮点或坏点
   - 本应是黑色区域的色偏

---

5. **Check from multiple angles:** Rotate the phone and view from 30-45 degree angles. Quality OLED maintains true black from all angles.

5. **从多个角度检查：** 旋转手机，从30-45度角观察。优质OLED从任何角度都能保持真正的纯黑。

---

### Red Flags to Watch For
### 需要警惕的危险信号

---

If you see any backlight glow on a screen sold as "OLED," you've been sold an LCD screen mislabeled as OLED. This is more common than you'd think—some suppliers deliberately mislabel Incell LCD screens as "Hard OLED" to command higher prices.

如果你在标称"OLED"的屏幕上看到任何背光发光，那你买到的就是被错误标注为OLED的LCD屏幕。这比你想象的更常见——一些供应商故意将Incell LCD屏幕错标为"硬性OLED"以获取更高价格。

---

Uniformity issues—where some areas appear darker or lighter than others—indicate manufacturing defects. A variance greater than 10% across the screen surface is unacceptable for quality repairs.

均匀性问题——某些区域看起来比其他区域更暗或更亮——表明存在制造缺陷。屏幕表面超过10%的差异对于高质量维修来说是不可接受的。

---

## How to Test for OLED Burn-in
## 如何检测OLED烧屏

---

Burn-in is the boogeyman of OLED technology. It's real, it happens, and it's one of the most common customer complaints about screen replacements. Here's how to test for it and understand the risks.

烧屏是OLED技术的心腹大患。它是真实存在的，确实会发生，也是屏幕更换后最常见的客户投诉之一。以下是如何检测烧屏并了解风险。

---

### What Causes OLED Burn-in?
### 什么导致OLED烧屏？

---

OLED burn-in occurs when pixels degrade unevenly. Pixels displaying bright, static content (like navigation bars or app icons) wear out faster than pixels showing varied content. Over time, this creates permanent "ghost images" on the screen.

当像素不均匀老化时，就会发生OLED烧屏。显示明亮静态内容（如导航栏或应用图标）的像素比显示变化内容的像素老化更快。随着时间推移，这会在屏幕上产生永久性的"残影"。

---

The good news? Modern iPhone OLED displays are engineered to minimize burn-in. Apple has implemented pixel-shifting algorithms and other technologies that significantly extend display lifespan.

好消息是什么？现代iPhone OLED显示屏被设计成能最大限度地减少烧屏。苹果实施了像素偏移算法和其他技术，显著延长了显示屏寿命。

---

### The Cetizen 510-Hour Test
### Cetizen 510小时测试

---

One of the most comprehensive burn-in studies was conducted by Cetizen, who tested the iPhone X against Samsung's Galaxy Note 8 and S7 Edge. They displayed the same static image on all three phones for 510 hours at full brightness.

最全面的烧屏研究之一是由Cetizen进行的，他们将iPhone X与三星Galaxy Note 8和S7 Edge进行了对比测试。他们在所有三部手机上以全亮度显示相同的静态图像510小时。

---

The results were telling:
- **iPhone X:** Minimal visible burn-in, best performance
- **Samsung S7 Edge:** Moderate burn-in
- **Samsung Note 8:** "Very visible burn-in"

结果很说明问题：
- **iPhone X：** 几乎看不到烧屏，表现最佳
- **三星S7 Edge：** 中度烧屏
- **三星Note 8：** "非常明显的烧屏"

---

This doesn't mean iPhone screens are immune—it means Apple's OLED implementation is more resistant. But aftermarket screens may not have the same protective technologies.

这并不意味着iPhone屏幕免疫烧屏——而是说明苹果的OLED实现更具抗烧屏能力。但第三方屏幕可能没有同样的保护技术。

---

### Quick Burn-in Detection Methods
### 快速烧屏检测方法

---

For testing incoming screen shipments, you won't have 510 hours. Here's a practical approach:

对于测试新到的屏幕货物，你没有510小时。以下是实用的方法：

---

1. **Gray screen test:** Display a uniform gray image (around 50% brightness). Burn-in shows up more clearly on gray than on black or white.

1. **灰屏测试：** 显示均匀的灰色图像（约50%亮度）。烧屏在灰色上比在黑色或白色上更容易显现。

---

2. **Solid color rotation:** Cycle through red, green, blue, and white screens. Look for any areas that appear different from the surrounding pixels.

2. **纯色轮换：** 在红、绿、蓝、白屏幕之间切换。查看是否有任何区域与周围像素看起来不同。

---

3. **Check common burn-in locations:** Status bar area, navigation bar area, and keyboard region are most susceptible.

3. **检查常见烧屏位置：** 状态栏区域、导航栏区域和键盘区域最容易出现烧屏。

---

4. **Use burn-in detection apps:** Apps like "OLED Saver" can help identify early-stage burn-in that's not visible to the naked eye.

4. **使用烧屏检测应用：** "OLED Saver"等应用可以帮助识别肉眼不可见的早期烧屏。

---

For bulk orders, request that suppliers provide screens that have passed burn-in testing. Reputable suppliers will have quality control processes that include this.

对于批量订单，要求供应商提供通过烧屏测试的屏幕。信誉良好的供应商会有包含这一项的质量控制流程。

## PWM Flicker Test - The Hidden Quality Killer
## PWM频闪测试——隐藏的质量杀手

---

This is where most repair shops fail their customers without even knowing it. PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) flicker is invisible to most people, but it causes real problems for a significant portion of the population.

这是大多数维修店在不知不觉中让客户失望的地方。PWM（脉宽调制）频闪对大多数人来说是不可见的，但它确实给相当一部分人群带来了问题。

---

### What is PWM and Why Should You Care?
### 什么是PWM，为什么你应该关心？

---

PWM is a method of controlling screen brightness. Instead of reducing the power to each pixel (DC dimming), PWM rapidly turns pixels on and off. At high brightness, the "on" periods are longer. At low brightness, the "off" periods dominate.

PWM是一种控制屏幕亮度的方法。它不是降低每个像素的功率（DC调光），而是快速开关像素。在高亮度时，"开"的时间更长。在低亮度时，"关"的时间占主导。

---

The problem? This creates flickering. While most people can't consciously see it, their eyes and brain still process it. For 10-15% of the population, this causes:
- Eye strain and fatigue
- Headaches, especially at low brightness
- Difficulty focusing
- General discomfort during extended use

问题是什么？这会产生闪烁。虽然大多数人无法有意识地看到它，但他们的眼睛和大脑仍然会处理它。对于10-15%的人群，这会导致：
- 眼睛疲劳和酸痛
- 头痛，尤其是在低亮度时
- 难以集中注意力
- 长时间使用时的普遍不适

---

Remember those three customers who returned screens complaining about eye strain? PWM sensitivity was likely the culprit.

还记得那三个因眼睛疲劳而退货的客户吗？PWM敏感很可能就是罪魁祸首。

---

### The Slow-Motion Camera Test
### 慢动作相机测试

---

Here's how to test for PWM issues:

以下是如何测试PWM问题：

---

1. **Set up your camera:** Use a phone with slow-motion video capability. 240fps or higher is ideal.

1. **设置相机：** 使用具有慢动作视频功能的手机。240fps或更高是理想的。

---

2. **Reduce screen brightness:** PWM is most pronounced at 25-50% brightness. Test at multiple levels.

2. **降低屏幕亮度：** PWM在25-50%亮度时最明显。在多个亮度级别测试。

---

3. **Record the screen:** Point your camera at the display and record for 10-15 seconds at each brightness level.

3. **录制屏幕：** 将相机对准显示屏，在每个亮度级别录制10-15秒。

---

4. **Review the footage:** Play back in slow motion. Look for visible flickering or banding across the screen.

4. **回放录像：** 以慢动作播放。查看屏幕上是否有可见的闪烁或条纹。

---

**What you're looking for:**
- **Good quality screen:** Minimal or no visible flicker, even at low brightness
- **Poor quality screen:** Obvious flickering, especially pronounced at lower brightness levels

**你要找的是：**
- **优质屏幕：** 即使在低亮度下也几乎没有或没有可见闪烁
- **劣质屏幕：** 明显的闪烁，尤其在较低亮度下更为明显

---

### PWM Frequency Benchmarks
### PWM频率基准

---

Not all PWM is created equal. The frequency matters:

并非所有PWM都是相同的。频率很重要：

---

- **Below 240Hz:** High risk of causing symptoms. Avoid these screens.
- **240-500Hz:** Moderate risk. May affect sensitive users.
- **500-1000Hz:** Lower risk, but some users may still be affected.
- **Above 3000Hz:** Generally considered safe for most users.

- **低于240Hz：** 高风险，容易引起症状。避免使用这类屏幕。
- **240-500Hz：** 中等风险。可能影响敏感用户。
- **500-1000Hz：** 较低风险，但一些用户可能仍会受影响。
- **高于3000Hz：** 通常被认为对大多数用户是安全的。

---

Original Apple OLED displays typically operate around 240Hz at low brightness, but Apple's implementation includes additional technologies to minimize the impact. Cheap aftermarket screens often have worse PWM characteristics.

原装苹果OLED显示屏在低亮度时通常以约240Hz运行，但苹果的实现包含了额外的技术来最小化影响。廉价的第三方屏幕往往具有更差的PWM特性。

---

### DC Dimming: The Alternative
### DC调光：另一种选择

---

Some higher-quality aftermarket screens offer DC dimming as an alternative to PWM. DC dimming reduces brightness by actually lowering the power to pixels, eliminating flicker entirely.

一些较高质量的第三方屏幕提供DC调光作为PWM的替代方案。DC调光通过实际降低像素功率来降低亮度，完全消除闪烁。

---

The trade-off? DC dimming can cause slight color accuracy issues at very low brightness levels. But for customers sensitive to PWM, it's a worthwhile compromise.

代价是什么？DC调光可能会在非常低的亮度级别下导致轻微的色彩准确度问题。但对于对PWM敏感的客户来说，这是值得的妥协。

---

When sourcing screens, ask suppliers specifically about PWM frequency and whether DC dimming is available.

采购屏幕时，具体询问供应商PWM频率以及是否提供DC调光。

---

## Color Accuracy and Brightness Testing
## 色彩准确度和亮度测试

---

Your customers may not know the technical specifications, but they'll immediately notice if colors look "off" compared to their friend's iPhone. Color accuracy testing ensures your repairs meet customer expectations.

你的客户可能不了解技术规格，但如果颜色看起来与朋友的iPhone不同，他们会立即注意到。色彩准确度测试确保你的维修达到客户期望。

---

### DisplayMate Standards
### DisplayMate标准

---

DisplayMate is the gold standard for display testing. Their testing of the original iPhone X found:
- **Color accuracy:** 0.9 JNCD (Just Noticeable Color Difference) - rated "Visually Indistinguishable From Perfect"
- **Peak brightness:** 634 nits (full screen), 809 nits (1% APL)
- **Contrast ratio:** Infinite (true blacks)

DisplayMate是显示测试的黄金标准。他们对原版iPhone X的测试发现：
- **色彩准确度：** 0.9 JNCD（刚可察觉色差）- 被评为"与完美视觉上无法区分"
- **峰值亮度：** 634尼特（全屏），809尼特（1% APL）
- **对比度：** 无限（真正的纯黑）

---

These are the benchmarks you're comparing against.

这些就是你对比的基准。

---

### Simple Color Accuracy Tests
### 简单的色彩准确度测试

---

You don't need a $50,000 spectroradiometer to test color accuracy. Here's a practical approach:

你不需要一台50000美元的分光辐射计来测试色彩准确度。以下是实用的方法：

---

1. **Side-by-side comparison:** Place the test screen next to a known-good original Apple display. Display the same images on both.

1. **并排对比：** 将测试屏幕放在已知良好的原装苹果显示屏旁边。在两边显示相同的图像。

---

2. **Skin tone test:** Human skin is incredibly difficult to reproduce accurately. Display photos of people and look for unnatural color casts.

2. **肤色测试：** 人类肤色非常难以准确再现。显示人物照片，查看是否有不自然的色偏。

---

3. **Gray ramp test:** Display a gradient from black to white. Look for color tinting (often green or pink) in the gray tones.

3. **灰阶测试：** 显示从黑到白的渐变。查看灰色调中是否有色偏（通常是绿色或粉色）。

---

4. **Primary color test:** Display pure red, green, and blue. Compare saturation and hue to a reference display.

4. **原色测试：** 显示纯红、绿、蓝色。与参考显示屏比较饱和度和色调。

---

**Acceptable tolerances:**
- Delta E (color difference) below 3 is acceptable for most repairs
- Delta E below 1 is excellent and matches original quality

**可接受的容差：**
- Delta E（色差）低于3对于大多数维修是可接受的
- Delta E低于1是优秀的，与原装质量匹配

---

### Brightness Uniformity Testing
### 亮度均匀性测试

---

Uneven brightness is a common defect in lower-quality screens. Test by:

亮度不均匀是低质量屏幕的常见缺陷。测试方法：

---

1. Display a solid white screen at maximum brightness

1. 以最高亮度显示纯白屏幕

---

2. Examine all areas of the screen, especially corners and edges

2. 检查屏幕所有区域，特别是角落和边缘

---

3. Look for darker or lighter patches

3. 查看是否有较暗或较亮的斑块

---

Variance greater than 10% across the screen surface indicates a manufacturing defect.

屏幕表面超过10%的差异表明存在制造缺陷。

### True Tone Verification
### 原彩显示验证

---

True Tone is Apple's ambient light adaptation feature. It requires proper sensor integration to function. After installing a replacement screen:

原彩显示是苹果的环境光适应功能。它需要正确的传感器集成才能工作。安装替换屏幕后：

---

1. Go to Settings > Display & Brightness

1. 进入设置 > 显示与亮度

---

2. Verify True Tone toggle is available

2. 验证原彩显示开关是否可用

---

3. Test in different lighting conditions

3. 在不同光照条件下测试

---

If True Tone is unavailable or non-functional, the screen may be missing proper IC chip programming or sensor integration.

如果原彩显示不可用或无法工作，屏幕可能缺少正确的IC芯片编程或传感器集成。

边缘手势对iOS导航至关重要：

### Ghost Touch Detection

Ghost touch—where the screen registers touches that didn't happen—is a serious defect:

幽灵触摸——屏幕注册未发生的触摸——是一个严重的缺陷：

任何幽灵触摸行为都是拒收的理由。

## 常见屏幕缺陷及如何识别

让我们用常见缺陷的快速参考指南来总结：
