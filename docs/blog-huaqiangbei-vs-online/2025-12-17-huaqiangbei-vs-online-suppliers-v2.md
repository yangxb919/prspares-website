### The "Unspoken Rules" of Huaqiangbei No One Will Voluntarily Tell You

Anyone who has spent a few years in Huaqiangbei knows these industry terms:

- **"Back-pressed Screens" (Refurbished/Reglassed)**: Replacing the glass cover of an original screen with a domestic one. Costs half as much, and is almost impossible to distinguish with the naked eye.
- **"Killing the Familiar" (Price Discrimination)**: Are you a new face? Congratulations, you pay 30% more for the exact same goods.
- **"Keyman"**: Every category has a few reliable stall owners, but they won't come looking for you; you need an introduction.
- **"Mixed Goods"**: A batch of goods mixed with screens of different grades. The good ones are on top for you to inspect, the bad ones are underneath when shipped to you.

**If you haven't squatted in Huaqiangbei for at least six months and don't know 2-3 reliable Keymen, I advise you not to go.**

## The Truth About Online Suppliers: Not All Are Reliable Either

Don't think I'm blindly praising online suppliers. There are plenty of trash suppliers in this industry too.

### How to Identify a Reliable Online Supplier?

**Look at these hard metrics:**

1. **RMA Rate (Return Rate)**
   - <1%: Excellent, can cooperate long-term
   - 1-3%: Passable, worth a try
   - >3%: Run, don't look back

2. **Is the Shipping Channel Duty-Paid (DDP)?**
   - **Reliable**: Provides DHL/FedEx lines, double customs clearance and tax included, 3-5 days direct to your shop, no customs worries for you.
   - **Unreliable**: Only cares about shipping, not customs. When goods arrive, you have to pay taxes and explain yourself, or even risk seizure.

3. **Do They Dare Show You Their Test Process?**
   - Dare: Shows they have confidence in their goods.
   - Don't Dare: Guess why?

4. **Payment Methods**
   - Supports PayPal/Credit Card: Disputes can be appealed.
   - Only accepts T/T (Wire Transfer): Once the money is gone, don't expect it back.
